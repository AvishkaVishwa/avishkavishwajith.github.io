12V AC to DC Converter (PCB)

AC to DC conversion using a transformer, bridge rectifier, LC filter, and linear regulation for clean DC output.

PowerPCBRectifierKiCad

Overview

This project converts 12V AC to a stable DC rail using classic power supply stages.

Goals

  • Clean DC output with low ripple
  • Simple and safe power stage layout
  • Board suitable for enclosure mounting

Power Stages

  • Step-down transformer and bridge rectifier
  • LC filtering for ripple reduction
  • Linear regulation for stable output

PCB Layout

  • Separation between AC input and DC output
  • Clear polarity markings and test points
  • Thermal spacing for regulator

Testing

Output voltage and ripple were checked under load to confirm stable DC delivery.

Results

The board produces a stable DC rail suitable for small electronics or prototype power needs.
