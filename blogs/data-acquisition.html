Data Acquisition System

ATmega328P data acquisition with MPU6050, DS18B20, and microphone sensors, visualized in LabVIEW.

ATmega328PLabVIEWSensorsDAQ

Overview

A compact DAQ solution that samples multiple sensors and streams data to LabVIEW for visualization.

Goals

  • Sample motion, temperature, and audio signals
  • Stream data reliably over serial
  • Display charts in real time

Hardware

  • ATmega328P microcontroller
  • MPU6050 IMU and DS18B20 temperature sensor
  • Microphone module for audio amplitude

Firmware and PC

  • Consistent sampling schedule
  • Structured serial packets
  • LabVIEW parsing and charting

Challenges and Fixes

Synchronizing sensor sampling and serial output required tuning the loop timing and packet format.

Results

The system provides a clear multi-sensor view and can be extended with additional channels.
